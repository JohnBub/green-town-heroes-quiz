Tour London on Wheels

London is a huge city with a lot of traffic. To solve this, they introduced a public bike-sharing system often called "Boris Bikes" (now Santander Cycles). It's a great example of sustainable transport.

How It Works

Docking Stations

There are hundreds of docking stations across the city where you can pick up or leave a bike.

The Commute

Many people use these bikes for their daily commute (the journey to work or school) because it's faster than sitting in a traffic jam.

Cost

It costs a small amount for access, but the first 30 minutes of every journey are usually free!

Note: Sustainable transport (transport durable) means ways of traveling that don't hurt the environment.
