Writing a Proposal

When you write a blog post or a letter about your town, follow this structure:

  1. Identify the Problem: "There is too much litter in the park."
  2. Make a Suggestion: "I think we should have more rubbish bins."
  3. Explain the Impact (First Conditional): "If we have more bins, people won't drop litter on the ground."
  4. Positive Ending: "To sum up, our town will be a better place for everyone!"
